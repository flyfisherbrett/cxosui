FAQ

  • question_answerWhy can't I see my company?

    Before you can view your company, you will need to connect CXOSoft to your QuickBooks Online company. You can accomplish this by clicking Add a Company in the left navigation, then click the Connect To QuickBooks button in the Add a Company dialog.

  • question_answerHow do I view change my other companies?

    From the left navigation select the company you wish to switch to from the Select Company menu.

  • question_answerHow do I set or change my NAICS code?

    From the left navigation select the company you wish to work with from the Select Company menu; then select Settings.
    In the Industry Code - NAICS box you can search for the NAICS code by name or 4 digit value that best matches your business.
    You might find this resource helpful to determine which code to use.

  • question_answerHow do I change my password?

    Click Edit Profile in the upper left square, then click Change Password.
    In the form that appears, enter your new password, confirm that password, and click Submit.

cash • flow

(noun.) – The total amount of money being transferred into and out of a business, especially as affecting liquidity.

liquidity

(noun.) – A measure of the extent to which a business has cash to meet immediate and short-term liabilities (who you owe), or assets (who owes you) that can be quickly converted to do this.
